Welcome to PitchPerfect!

Never pitched an idea? Nervous speaking infront of crowds? Join the PitchPerfect Club to help you learn Public Speaking and effective Pitching skills! PitchPerfect pairs you with an expert who understands the nuances of creating a strong pitch and telling a compelling about your product. Step into Virtual Reality to practice your pitch infront of live judges and receive real time feedback.

For startup founders, hackathon attendees and anyone looking to erase their fear of public speaking and dramatically improve their ability to communicate effectively.

If you have a product and need pitching feedback before demo day come see PitchPerfect now in the South Hall! By immersing you in simulated pitching situations with access to realtime expert feedback from event judges, PitchPerfect will improve your public speaking ability exponentially BEFORE your big event.
